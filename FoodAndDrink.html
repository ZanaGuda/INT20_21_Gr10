Best Places to eat in Kosovo

Pristina, Kosovo Restaurants

  1. Soma Book Station

    2. Soma Book Station Soma Book Station is a favorite with locals and tourists alike. The passionate staff, centralized bar, and impressive display of books and vinyl records draw the eye. Readers can rent books for 1 Euro, and vinyl records can be played or exchanged. Their collection includes the likes of Chet Baker, Sonny Rollins, John Cage, The Fall, and so on. The drinks range from rare wines to local craft beverages that change with the seasons. And the food is simple yet tasteful. If you only go to one place in Kosovo, it has to be the Soma Book Station.

    • Adults only
  2. Restaurant Liburnia

    3. Restaurant LiburniaA short distance from the Pristina Townhall, the Restaurant Liburnia looks like something from a fairytale. Tangled vines, lamps, and folk music greet your entry to this Albanian restaurant. Must-try dishes include in-house bread and slow-cooked goat.

    • Old style Restaurant
  3. Tiffany

    4. Tiffany Tourists love the sunny terrace at Tiffany as much as they love organic food and drink here. Bread is baked fresh at the restaurant and served with the day’s choice of meats and vegetables. You can also buy some delicious in-house-made Ajvar as a memento of your trip to Kosovo.

    • Recommended for people who prefere rock music

Best Restaurants In Kosovo, Peja

  1. Kulla e Zenel Beut

    2. The Restaurant Kulla e Zenel Beut with its stone walls looks like it’s come right out of the 18th or 19th century. It’s also ideally located in the heart of town with a lovely terrace. Taves are the must-try item here.

    • Old Restaurant
  2. Hotel Dukagjini

    3. One of Pec’s leading hotels, Hotel Dukagjini, is situated on the Lumi i Bardhe River in the heart of Pec and has been serving customers since 1956. Go here to try delicious local food or even spend a night here.

    • Family place

Gjakova, Kosovo Restaurants

  1. Hani i Vjetër

    2. Dark timber walls and furniture contrast with an airy courtyard. You can choose to eat indoors or outdoors at Hani i Vjetër and enjoy a range of traditional dishes, including fish. Hani i Haraçisë Hani i Haraçisë is one of the oldest restaurants in Gjakova and was previously a caravanserai serving weary travelers. Wooden logs can be seen stacked neatly along the walls, and their delicious taves now delight locals and tourists.

    • Recommended for people who prefere wine

Best restaurants in Kosovo, Prizren

  1. Best Restaurants In Kosovo, Prizren

    2. Restaurant Marasahi Close to the Marasahi trail, set by an old sycamore tree, the Restaurant Marasahi serves traditional Albanian dishes. However, the best part is getting the chance to cook your steaks on hot stones before eating them.

    • Recommended for people who prefere traditional food
  2. Fish House

    3. For a welcome break from everyday Kosovan food, the Fish House in Prizren is the best chance. Tastefully prepared dishes of fish and other seafood even delight the locals.

    • Best resturant for Seafood in Prizren
  3. Ambient Restaurant

    4. The name says it all. Situated close to the Prizren Fortress, just next to the river overlooking the old town, Ambient Restaurant is the perfect place for a quiet dinner. You can choose to sit indoors in winter or enjoy sitting outside on warm summer nights.

    • Offers beautiful view

    Other Drinks To Try In Kosovo

  1. Boza: A sweet malted corn drink that goes well with pastries.
  2. Kos: A yogurt made from goat’s milk.
  3. Ayran: A yogurt drink that is usually drunk as an accompaniment for pastries.
  4. Tursha Kafa: A thick, sweet and strong Turkish coffee.
  5. Kompot: A sweet non-alcoholic beverage made by cooking local fruit with sugar and flavored with cinnamon or vanilla. It’s served hot during autumn.
  6. Rasoj: A juice of fermented red cabbage that’s popular in winter.
